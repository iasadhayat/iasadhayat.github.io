About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher at Taiyuan University of Science and Technology, China, focusing on computational intelligence and optimization techniques. My work bridges the gap between theoretical algorithm design and real-world engineering applications.

My research interests include surrogate modeling, evolutionary computation, dimensionality reduction, and applying machine learning to solve complex optimization problems.
